# Design Brief

## Style: Electric Dream

The design should evoke a sense of excitement and energy, with neon-lit cityscapes and pulsating lights that transport users to a world of immersive music experiences. The AR elements should blend seamlessly with real-world environments, creating an otherworldly atmosphere that's both futuristic and captivating. The overall aesthetic should be bold, dynamic, and mesmerizing, with interactive elements that invite users to explore and engage with the virtual performances.

## Design Tokens

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| Colors | Bright and bold, with a focus on neon hues and deep blues |
| Typography | Futuristic and edgy, with a mix of sans-serif and script fonts |
| Components | Sleek and modern, with glowing accents and rounded edges |
| Imagery | High-energy, with a mix of concert photography and futuristic illustrations |

## Theme Settings

- **Theme Color:** violet
- **Mode:** dark
- **Border Radius:** 0.5

## App Variant: SoundScout Explore

> Uncover Hidden Music Gems

This variant is designed for adventurous music fans, offering a gamified experience that encourages users to explore new music and discover emerging artists. SoundScout Explore uses AI-generated playlists and AR-powered scavenger hunts to guide users through a curated selection of music, rewarding them with exclusive content and virtual badges. By leveraging gamification elements, this variant makes music discovery a fun and engaging experience.

**Target Audience:** Adventurous music fans and gamers
**Monetization:** In-app purchases for premium content and virtual items
**Complexity:** mvp
